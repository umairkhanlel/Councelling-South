COUNSELLING SOUTH

Counselling South is an established centre offering professional counselling, psychotherapy and psychological services south of the river as well as in Cottesloe, Western Australia.

We provide a welcoming, respectful and supportive environment where individuals, couples, and families can resolve their difficulties.

At Counselling South our therapeutic approaches emphasise the importance of the quality of the relationship between therapist and client as the basis of a positive and fulfilling outcome for our clients.

Should you have any questions about Counselling South and the services we provide, or require assistance choosing a practitioner suited to your needs, we welcome your enquiries.
